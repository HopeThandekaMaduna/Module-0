Best places to be in Stellenbosch

Cavalli Estate

Surrounded by views of the Helderberg mountain range to the east and Cape Town’s city skyline etched on the west, Cavalli boasts some of the best views in Stellenbosch. A must-visit destination in the Cape Winelands. Wine tasting: Wednesday to Sunday from 10h00 – 18h00. More information: www.cavalliestate.com



Hidden Valley Wines

With breathtaking views as far as the eye can see, this Stellenbosch gem takes full advantage of its elevated surroundings. Hidden Valley Wines is the perfect place to immerse yourself in nature, take in the vines and olive grove and enjoy the best of modern South African dining.



Waterkloof

To experience Waterkloof is to discover the very pinnacle of views in Stellenbosch. Their ‘cellar in the sky’ is set atop the Schaapenberg Hills, with endless views of the Hottentots-Holland mountain range and False Bay coastline. This one-of-a-kind property is the perfect place to mingle with friends.



Delaire Graff Estate

The moment you set foot on Delaire Graff Estate you know you’re in for an aesthetic treat. Perched at the top of Stellenbosch’s Helshoogte Pass and overlooking the awe-inspiring Banhoek Valley, Delaire’s world-renowned art collection blends perfectly with their wine, gardens and dramatic mountain setting.
